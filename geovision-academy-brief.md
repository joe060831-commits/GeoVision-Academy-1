# GeoVision Academy — Website Design Brief

## 1. Brand Identity

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| Brand Name | GeoVision Academy |
| Tagline | "Mapping Knowledge, Shaping Futures" |
| Industry | Online Education — GIS, Geology, AI, and Digital Skills Training |
| Geographic Focus | Pan-African, with global accessibility |
| Brand Positioning | A credible, investable EdTech startup — not a hobby project |

## 2. Design Direction

**Style:** Premium university-style website; modern, responsive, and clean, with a professional academic layout suitable for students, professionals, investors, and institutional partners.

**Color Palette:** Blue, green, and white — blue conveying trust and technology, green representing earth sciences and growth, white providing clarity and openness.

**Visual Influences:** GIS and mapping interfaces, geological strata and terrain, academic institutions, and modern AI/tech platforms.

**Motion & Interaction:** Subtle, modern animations (fade-ins, hover states, scroll reveals) that reinforce credibility without feeling gimmicky.

## 3. Logo Specification

A single mark combining:
- A globe or Earth outline as the base form
- Overlaid map grid or GIS coordinate lines
- A mountain silhouette integrated into or beneath the globe, representing geology
- A color treatment in blue and green only
- An overall finish that reads as academic and technology-forward (avoid clip-art or overly literal styling)

**Placement:** Navbar (left-aligned, paired with wordmark) and footer (centered or left-aligned, paired with tagline).

## 4. Target Audience

- Students (secondary and university level)
- Practicing geologists and GIS professionals
- Universities and academic institutions
- Employers and hiring partners
- Investors and potential funding partners

## 5. Site Architecture

### 5.1 Home Page

**Navigation:** Logo, primary menu (Home, Courses, Certificate Verification, About, Contact), and a persistent CTA button.

**Hero Section**
- Headline: "GeoVision Academy"
- Tagline: "Mapping Knowledge, Shaping Futures"
- Description: "An online platform for GIS, Geology, AI, and Digital Skills education across Africa."
- Primary CTA: "Explore Courses"
- Secondary CTA: "Join Waitlist"

**Featured Courses** — preview cards linking to the Courses page.

**Why Choose Us** — key differentiators (affordability, practical skills, AI-assisted learning, African context).

**Statistics Section** — quantified credibility markers (e.g., learners reached, courses offered, partner institutions).

**Student Dashboard Preview** — mockup of the learner experience (progress tracking, course access, AI tutor entry point).

**Certificate Preview** — visual sample of an earned certificate, linking to the verification page.

**Testimonials Section** — learner and partner quotes.

**Footer** — logo, tagline, sitemap links, social channels, contact information, and copyright.

### 5.2 Courses Page

Display each course as a card with: title, short description, duration, skill level, and a "Coming Soon" badge where applicable.

| Course | Status |
|---|---|
| Introduction to GIS | Available |
| Basic Geology | Available |
| AI for Students | Available |
| Digital Skills Fundamentals | Available |
| Remote Sensing Fundamentals | Coming Soon |

### 5.3 Certificate Verification Page

- Certificate ID search field
- "Verify Certificate" button
- Verification result state showing validity status
- Sample record for demonstration:
  - Student Name: John Doe
  - Course: Introduction to GIS
  - Certificate ID: GVA-GIS-0001
- Modern, branded certificate preview with a download option

### 5.4 About Us Page

**Mission:** "To provide affordable, practical, and technology-driven education that empowers African learners with geospatial, geological, AI, and digital skills."

**Vision:** "To become Africa's leading online academy for GIS, Geology, AI, and emerging technologies."

**Sections:**
- Founder Story — origin and motivation behind GeoVision Academy
- Academic Partnerships — current and prospective institutional collaborations
- Future Roadmap — milestones for courses, partnerships, and growth

## 6. Cross-Site Features

- Fully responsive layout (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Modern, restrained animations across page transitions and interactive elements
- Consistent, professional navigation menu across all pages
- Student dashboard mockup (Home page)
- Certificate download preview (Certificate Verification page)
- AI Tutor feature showcase (Home page or dedicated section)
- Waitlist registration form (Home page, with confirmation state)

## 7. Success Criteria

The finished site should read as a credible, fundable EdTech product — polished enough to present to academic partners and investors, and clear enough to convert first-time visitors into students or waitlist signups.

About GeoVision Academy

Education shaped by the ground it's built on

We're building the academy we wished existed — practical, affordable, and designed for African learners from the first lesson.

Mission

"To provide affordable, practical, and technology-driven education that empowers African learners with geospatial, geological, AI, and digital skills."

Vision

"To become Africa's leading online academy for GIS, Geology, AI, and emerging technologies."

Founder Story

Why GeoVision Academy exists

GeoVision Academy began with a simple observation: across Liberia and the wider region, talented students and working professionals wanted to learn GIS, geology, AI, and digital skills — but the courses available to them were either too expensive, built for a different context entirely, or simply didn't exist online.

Founder Joe B.S. Robinson Jr. set out to close that gap: a platform that pairs practical, locally grounded coursework with AI-powered support, so that geography and budget are no longer the reason a promising learner gets left behind.
